Fireside Chat with Rich Miner, co-founder of Android

Career Inspiration Series: Rich Miner and Bill Aulet, Fireside Chat 

DATE: 05/09/2024 

TIME: 5:00-6:00pm ET (with food) - RSVP
ROOM: 45-230

 

Rich Miner is the co-founder of Android. After being acquired by Google, Miner led Android development and helped create GV, Google's venture capital arm, and also served as Googles chief technology officer of Android Tablets. Boston Globe also named Rich one of "the most powerful tech leaders in Boston." 

 

Rich will be joined by Bill Aulet, Managing Director, Martin Trust Center for MIT Entrepreneurship for a fireside chat on his career in entrepreneurship.

 

Hosted by the EECS Alliance and the Martin Trust Center for MIT Entrepreneurship
